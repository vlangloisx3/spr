Thanks for participating in our study!

This is a research study about normal language use.

There are three parts to this study, which takes approximately around 15-20 minutes to complete.

  1. Background questionnaire
  2. Practice
  3. Reading task

Requirements:

  • You must be 18 years or older.
  • You must be a native speaker of English.
  • You must do this task in one sitting without interruptions.
  • You can only do this task once.
